Hello! I'm Charlie Harding

I’m a software engineer for QAware, based in Munich, Germany. I studied Computer Science at the University of Bristol.

My main technical interests are in frontend development and digital mapping. In my free time, I like spending time in the mountains: hiking, running, road cycling and skiing.

My Projects

  • Strava Heatmapper — a tool for viewing all recorded Strava activities in a heatmap, with options for filtering activities. This uses Mapbox and the Strava API.
  • Hiking with Charlie — a quick view of all hikes I am organising/have organised through Hiking Buddies. Originally created in order to provide short links with improved link previews for social media.
  • UBES Walks — a resource for members of the University of Bristol Expeditions Society to find accessible local walks during the corona lockdowns of 2020-21.
  • AirGestures — an app created for the ICHack 2018 as part of Team Limoncello. It allows a user to control their computer using gestures in the air.
  • charding.dev — this site: a static site using its own site builder written in Ruby and built on top of HAML.
